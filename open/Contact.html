Emergency Contact information

It is important to know these important contacts incase of any emergency when traveling to anywhere in the United Kingdom not jsut exclusively to London:

  • To contact emergency services such as Police, Ambulance, Fire Service, dial 999 or 112 into your phone. Both numbers serve the same purpose and connects to the same services, the only difference between the two is that 112 can be used across the EU. Also if you wish to contact emergency services without talking or silently as you think it may put you more in danger press 55 and emergency services will be sent to assist you.

  • For information in regards to information and advice on the current healt conditions in the United Kingdom, for example if there are any restrictions due to COVID-19, you can visit the National Health Service Website (NHS) as well as call 111 if you need urgent medical help if it is not life threatening.

  • If you or someone you are traveling to the UK with is missing and do not know their whereabouts you can call the local police by dialing 101. Attached below is documentation provided by the UK Missing Persons Unit.
