UK Customs and Regulations

To summarize, the United Kingdom allows foreigners to bring any kind of goods from abroad and will not have to worry about paying a duty or tax aslong as they are for personal use or if they are to be gifted to someone. Though not all items are permitted into the country.
The following items are not allowed in the United Kingdom per GOV.UK:

  • Controlled Drugs
  • Offensive weapons
  • Self-defence sprays
  • Endangered Animals and Plant Species
  • Rough Diamonds
  • Indecent and Obscene Materials
  • Personal imports of meat and dairy from most non-eu contries
